Welcome X-mask

Welcome to the X-mask. The X-mask has a gateway and wallet function to use XRUN's main net coin and NFT. With a Chrome browser, you can use both your crypto assets and wallet functions. This wallet has the following functions:

- Create your own wallet and load your already created XRunMainnet wallet
- Transfer of X-Run Mainnet Tokens
- Send Token to Exchange
- Confirmation of metaverse items and acquisition of tokens for purchase
- Personalization of Wallet
